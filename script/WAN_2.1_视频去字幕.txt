
# 上传并加载 WAN2.1_视频去字幕.json 工作流

# 按照提示下载工作流节点
1、让deepseek协助找到这些节点的git地址
2、在git页面直接下载，然后放在/root/comfyui/storage/ComfyUI/custom_nodes目录下

text_encoders（10g）
wget -P ~/comfyui/storage-models/models/text_encoders/ https://hf-mirror.com/Comfy-Org/Wan_2.1_ComfyUI_repackaged/resolve/main/split_files/text_encoders/umt5_xxl_fp16.safetensors

VAE（240M）
wget https://huggingface.co/Kijai/WanVideo_comfy/resolve/main/Wan2_1_VAE_bf16.safetensors

diffusion_model(2.1g)
https://huggingface.co/Kijai/WanVideo_comfy/blob/main/Wan2_1-MiniMaxRemover_1_3B_fp16.safetensors

loras(700M)
https://huggingface.co/lightx2v/Wan2.1-I2V-14B-480P-StepDistill-CfgDistill-Lightx2v/blob/main/loras/Wan21_I2V_14B_lightx2v_cfg_step_distill_lora_rank64.safetensors
